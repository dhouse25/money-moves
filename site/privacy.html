Money Move$

Privacy

Last updated: July 9, 2026 · Written for teens, parents, and schools.

Short version: Money Move$ is a free educational game. Progress is saved on your device. We don’t sell kids’ data. We don’t require a credit card. We don’t run stranger chat.

What we collect (today)

  • On your device only: game progress, streaks, ranks, and settings (browser localStorage).
  • Optional email: if you join the waitlist, that address is stored so we can send the free 7-day plan and product updates. You can stop anytime.
  • Anonymous usage events: button clicks and level completes may be counted so we know what works. These use a random session id — not your name.

What we do not do

  • No credit card required to play.
  • No loot boxes or pay-to-win for minors.
  • No selling personal information to data brokers.
  • No open chat with strangers.
  • No tracking kids across other apps for ads (we are not an ad network).

Teens & children

Money Move$ is designed for middle-schoolers through adults. If you are under 13 in the U.S., please use the game with a parent or guardian. Parents can review progress on the same device. We aim to follow the spirit of COPPA and school privacy norms: minimal data, no shady monetization of kids.

School or credit-union pilots will get a separate data agreement before any account system or class roster features go live.

Third-party links

The toolkit links out to banks, brokers, apps, and insurance sites. Those sites have their own privacy policies. We may later use clearly labeled affiliate links; if we earn a referral fee, it will never change the educational advice on this site.

Your controls

  • Clear game data anytime in the game (Trophy → reset progress) or by clearing site data in your browser.
  • To remove a waitlist email, email us from that address and ask to be deleted (contact on the site footer when available).

Changes

If we add accounts, payments, or school dashboards, we will update this page before those features collect new personal data.
