Questions parents ask

Is it free?

Yes. The core game and lessons are free forever. If we ever add a parent Family plan, it will be optional, clearly labeled, and never required to learn the basics.

Is it appropriate for middle school?

Yes. Players pick Middle School, High School, College, or Young Adult so examples match their life (allowance vs rent). You can sit with them on level 1 — it takes about a minute.

Will it push crypto, day trading, or gambling?

No. We actively teach traps to dodge — get-rich-quick schemes, whole life as an “investment,” and commission-driven products. The core move is boring on purpose: low-cost index funds + time.

Do you collect my kid’s data?

Progress is stored on the device. Optional email is only if someone joins the waitlist. Read the full Privacy page.

How do I get them to start?

Text them the game link and race them on level 1. Or open it together on the couch. First win unlocks the climb — that’s the hook.
